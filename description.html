Exploratory Analytic Tool: Barometric Pressure Changes By Hour

- Interact with the widgets on the left to build a scatter plot of a US Weather Stations barometric pressure volatility.
- Toggle by year, station as well as hourly comparison period: 3 Hour Lag, 6 Hour Lag or 24 Hour Lag.

- Inspired by the Bokeh Interactive Apps on GitHub. (Information is sourced from NOAA Data Records.)
