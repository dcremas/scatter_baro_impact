Exploratory Scatter Plots: Barometric Pressure Volatility By Hour

- Interact with the widgets on the left to build a scatter plot of select US Weather Stations barometric pressure volatility.
- Toggle by year, station, as well as hourly comparison period: 3 Hour Lag, 6 Hour Lag or 24 Hour Lag.
- Each dot represents the pressure change versus the hour change period (LAG) selected, for all hours within a year.
- Information is sourced from NOAA Data Records.
